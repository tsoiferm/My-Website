Student, Data Enthusiast, and Published Researcher

Hi there, thanks for visiting my website! I’m Tyler Soiferman; I am a Global Technology Analyst at Bank of America and a Computer Science graduate student at Stevens Institute of Technology, concentrating in machine learning and management.

Over the last several years, I’ve gained valuable experience in Data Science/Analytics and Computer Science in general. My past experience includes streamlining and automating a crucial analytics process during my internship with Bank of America; publishing a natural language processing research project while interning at the New Zealand based Dot Loves Data; and evaluating time series models’ abilities to forecast labor market metrics when trained on document embeddings as a student researcher at Stevens. These past endeavors have helped me develop my abilities and expertise within the Data Science field.

At Stevens, I’ve had the opportunity to serve as a leader, during my time on the Dean’s Student Advisory Board for the School of Business and the Stevens Computer Science Student Advisory Council, and as a Global Ambassador for the Study Abroad program. On top of that, I always ensure to make time to volunteer at my local library, as I’ve done for over eight years.

I’m always excited to work alongside a talented array of diverse individuals who share my passion for Data Science and Analytics! Please feel free to check out my portfolio!
